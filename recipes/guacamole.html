Guacamole

Description

You can make this avocado salad in onle 10 min, have 4 servings.

You can make it smooth or chunky depending on your tastes

Ingredients

  • 3 avocados - peeled, pitted, and mashed
  • 1 lime, juiced
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • Â½ cup diced onion
  • 3 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 3 roma (plum) tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon minced garlic
  • 1 pinch ground cayenne pepper (Optional)

Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, mash together the avocados, lime juice, and salt. Mix in onion, cilantro, tomatoes, and garlic.

    Stir in cayenne pepper. Refrigerate 1 hour for best flavor, or serve immediately.

  2. Enjoy!
